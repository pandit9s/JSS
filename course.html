COURSES OFFERED

The courses offered in the JSS are a blend of technical and employability skills. This enables the beneficiaries in learning the right set of skills to be utilised for gainful self/wage employment. The Directorate of JSS (DJSS) has developed suitable job roles for the JSS clientele. The National Council for Vocational Education and Training (NCVET) has approved 15 Qualifications / Courses for the JSS scheme, which have been aligned with NSQF levels 2 and 3.

Further, in view of the local market demand and geographical requirements, and to cater the beneficiaries with suitable courses, 35 more NSQF-aligned courses of level 2 have been adopted for training during FY 2023-24.

JSSs now offer 9 courses, which have local market demand in the field. The said 9 courses have domain skills knowledge of 135-360 hours and employability skills of 60 hours. The sector-wise details of the courses being offered under the JSS scheme are as below:

Organised Retail
Apparel, Made Ups and Home Furnishing
Beauty and Wellness
Electronics and Hardware
Food Processing
IT-ITES
Handicrafts and Carpet
Textiles and Handlooms
Handicrafts and Carpet
